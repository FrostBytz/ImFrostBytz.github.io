Our Story

Composing layouts, one line of code at a time

My name is Drew Jacob. Your next front-end web developer. I am passionate about web design, and I enjoy all the little intricacies that exist within the word of tech. My goal is to learn and progress as much as the world grants me possible. Beyond front-end development, I have full-stack in my sights. Back-end is something I am currently learning. Additionally, outside of webdevelopment-I am developing a 2D platformer that I produce all the assets for, music included. I use ableton, krita and godot engine to piece together a lovely pass time. Being on the computer screen and writing out code-working towards something I once thought impossible-drives me to get out of bed every day. I dont go into my days as if they were an accident. I will stop at nothing until I achieve my dreams. I wish to learn as many programming languages I am able. I wish to further develop my knowledge of arithmatic geometry. One day I will even put out a fully developed videogame of my own.

My goal is to help encapsulate the dream of what it is to create/design content other people enjoy and associate with something creative.

Let us help each other create something to hold onto forever and be able look back on after we climbed the latter of success.
